Our Story

Batrick Carnegie and Bamber Carnegie created Bun Bun Bakery in 1995, out of their passions for food, friendship and hard work.

From the start they baked everything fresh on-site each day and donated the day’s leftovers to charity each night, actions that remain solidly in place almost 25 years later.

We take our name from Chinese word “Bang,” meaning good and well-being.
